Darth Vader Tribute

Here is a timeline of Dareth Vader's life:


  • 41BBY Born Anakin Skywalker on the planet Tatooine
  • 32BBY Anakin wins Boonta Eve Classic pod race, the only human to ever win
  • 32BBY Leaves Tatooine to become Jedi padawan learner
  • 32BBY While hiding on a Naboo starfighter he takes off into space and destroys the Trade Federation ship ending an invasion
  • 32BBY Begins Jedi training under Master Obi-Wan Kenobi
  • 22BBY Participates in first Battle of Geonosis, looses right arm in a lightsabre battle with Count Dooku
  • 22BBY Marries Padame Amidala in a secret ceremony on Naboo
  • 22BBY Clone Wars begin
  • 19BBY Defeats Count Dooku
  • 19BBY Turns to the dark side of the force and becomes Darth Vader under his new master Emperor Palpatine
  • 19BBY Travels to the Mustafar system to end clone wars.
  • 19BBY Battles former Jedi master Obi-Wan Kenobi on Mustfar and is defeated losing his legs and left arm, he is then fitted with cybornetic arms and legs
  • 19BBY Unbeknownst to him his wife Padame gives birth to two children Luke and Leia before she dies
  • 0BBY Is one of the few survivors when the first Death Star is destroyed in the Battle of Yavin
  • 3ABY Invades the Hoth system to attempt to defeat the rebel forces
  • 3ABY Learns that Like is his son and attempts and fails to capture Luke on Cloud City to turn him to the dark side
  • 4ABY Captures Luke on Endor and brings him to new Death Star
  • 4ABY Has epic lightsaber duel with Luke after Luke refuses to turn to the dark side. After being defeted by Luke he returned to the light side of the force to save his son from Palpatine
  • 4ABY Dies after defeating Palpatine on the second Death Star in the Endor system
