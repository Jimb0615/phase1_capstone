About Me


Hi, my name is Jim Brown and I am a Principal Systems Engineer on the Infrastructure team within Liberty Mutual Investments IT. I began my career with Liberty in 2008 as an end user support technician for the Investments department. Over the years my role has changes from end user support to second level support and for the last 13 years I have been responsible for the operation of our data center and back end servers.

Prior to Liberty I worked for Bose Corporation where I was the senior desktop architect. I was responsible for the standards of the desktop compute enviornment as well as automating standardized OS and software installs for the company world wide.

Outside of work I have varied interests. I like to travel, ride motorcycles on and off road, fish, hike with my dogs, photography, cook especially BBQ and grilling, fix/build things. More recently I have taken interest in off grid solar installations and have immersed myself in learning everyting i can about them. Using thay new knowledge I have built a solar power and energy storage system that will enable us to live off grid in our RV for extended periods.
